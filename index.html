Mancala AI

Mancala is a classic strategy game. Play against a friend or test your skill against an AI powered by MiniMax with alpha-beta pruning.

How to Play Mancala

  • The board has 2 rows of 6 pits and 2 stores (one for each player).
  • Each pit starts with 4 stones.
  • Players take turns selecting one of their pits to distribute stones counter-clockwise.
  • If the last stone lands in your store, you get another turn.
  • If the last stone lands in your empty pit and the opposite pit has stones, you capture both into your store.
  • The game ends when one sideâ€™s pits are empty.
  • Remaining stones on the opponent's side go to their store.
  • The player with the most stones in their store wins.
